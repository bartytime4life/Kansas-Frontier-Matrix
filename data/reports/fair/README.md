## üìò Overview
`data/reports/fair/` contains all **FAIR+CARE validation outputs**, **ethics assessments**, and **council reviews** for KFM datasets.  
Reports quantify KFM‚Äôs commitment to **open data**, **ethical AI**, and **transparent governance**.

**v10 Enhancements**
- **Telemetry v2**: energy/CO‚ÇÇ and validation-coverage metrics alongside FAIR/CARE results.  
- **Streaming STAC** cross-links: continuous catalog updates reflected in report metadata.

- Documents dataset compliance with FAIR+CARE standards.  
- Records metrics for council review and governance dashboards.  
- Links outcomes to datasets, manifests, and audit ledgers.  
- Is generated by `.github/workflows/faircare-validate.yml` and ledger sync jobs.

## ‚öôÔ∏è FAIR+CARE Workflow
```mermaid
flowchart TD
    "Dataset Validation Completed" --> "FAIR Evaluation (Findable ¬∑ Accessible ¬∑ Interoperable ¬∑ Reusable)"
    "FAIR Evaluation (Findable ¬∑ Accessible ¬∑ Interoperable ¬∑ Reusable)" --> "CARE Assessment (Collective Benefit ¬∑ Authority ¬∑ Responsibility ¬∑ Ethics)"
    "CARE Assessment (Collective Benefit ¬∑ Authority ¬∑ Responsibility ¬∑ Ethics)" --> "Generate FAIR & CARE Reports"
    "Generate FAIR & CARE Reports" --> "Governance Council Review + Ledger Update"
```

### Process Steps
1. **FAIR Evaluation** ‚Äî Automated checks: metadata completeness, schema conformance, STAC discoverability.  
2. **CARE Assessment** ‚Äî Combined manual/automated review of ethics, governance, and community impact.  
3. **Scorecard Generation** ‚Äî Metrics consolidated in `faircare_scorecard.csv`.  
4. **Governance Review** ‚Äî Council signs off; `data/reports/audit/data_provenance_ledger.json` updated.
