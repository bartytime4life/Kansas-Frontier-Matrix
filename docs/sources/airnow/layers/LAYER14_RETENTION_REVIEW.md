# AirNow Layer 14 Retention Review

Internal retention-review intake only.

Layer 14 reads local Layer 13 retention-planning artifacts and local manual review evidence, then emits deterministic review-intake artifacts for internal archival-review readiness.

- No live ingestion.
- No downloads.
- No web-service calls.
- No ZIP-code web-service loops.
- No command execution.
- No file deletion/destruction.
- No archive transfer or external storage action.
- No chmod/chattr/immutable flag action.
- No legal hold creation.
- No official archive certification.
- No legal advice.
- Not publication output / dashboard / tiles / public API.
- Not emergency alerts.
- Not regulatory analysis.

AirNow data are preliminary and subject to change. Official regulatory data must come from EPA AQS/AirData.
