**Purpose:**  
Provide a formal mechanism for **Indigenous Nations, descendant communities, or cultural authorities** to **withdraw previously granted consent** for the publication, visualization, distribution, or AI interpretation of sensitive cultural or archaeological data.  
This revocation framework safeguards **Indigenous Data Sovereignty**, **CARE Principles**, and **ethical stewardship** under **MCP-DL v6.3**.

## üìò When This Form Is Used

This form is submitted when a Tribal Nation or cultural authority **revokes earlier approval** for:

- Public datasets or metadata  
- Maps, dashboards, or spatial visualizations  
- Temporal timelines or historical narratives  
- AI-generated interpretations, summaries, or embeddings  
- Story-node or narrative-driven products  
- Any representation that relies on **sensitive Indigenous or cultural data**

Revocation may occur **at any time** and must be immediately honored.
