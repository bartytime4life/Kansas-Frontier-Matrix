## üéØ Objective

The **AI Assistant** serves as KFM‚Äôs intelligent layer for exploration, discovery, and contextual synthesis.  
It bridges the gap between human questions and the graph‚Äôs structured data ‚Äî enabling **natural-language querying**, **contextual summarization**, and **semantic storytelling** with explainable and reproducible AI methods.

## üß† Cognitive & UX Guidelines

- Keep AI outputs concise; limit paragraphs ‚â§ 3 lines.  
- Highlight important entities using bold and accessible colors.  
- Use ‚Äúconfidence chips‚Äù rather than colored bars for readability.  
- All AI text must be user-copyable and screen-reader compatible.  
- Every generated response includes visible citations and model metadata.  

---

## ü§ñ AI Transparency Policy

> All AI responses must include:
> - Confidence level (High / Medium / Low).  
> - List of cited data sources (linked to STAC entries).  
> - Disclosure of AI model name, version, and data scope.  
> - Visible toggle to disable or hide AI-generated summaries.  

‚úÖ **Why:** Complies with MCP-DL ¬ß10 ‚ÄúEthical Automation and Provenance Reporting.‚Äù
