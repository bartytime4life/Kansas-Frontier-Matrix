# ‚è±Ô∏èü™∂‚è≥ **Sovereignty Masking Propagation ‚Äî Temporal Entity Governance Test Plan**  
`‚Ä¶/entities/temporal_entities/README.md`

**Purpose:**  
Define the v11 governance test plan that ensures **all temporal entities** (instants, intervals, eras, historical periods, ceremonial cycles, tribal chronology bands, etc.) are governed under **sovereignty-safe masking protections**, preventing:

- Exposure of exact times for protected cultural events  
- Reconstruction of tribal/heritage timelines  
- Inference of ceremonial calendars or temporal rhythms  
- Drift-based revelation of restricted temporal structure  

Temporal masking must remain intact across:

- The Knowledge Graph (OWL-Time, CIDOC-CRM)  
- Embeddings & latent temporal features  
- Clustering & anomaly detectors  
- Story Node v3 narratives  
- Focus Mode v3 temporal reasoning  
- STAC/DCAT temporal metadata  
- PROV-O & OpenLineage temporal provenance

# üß© Temporal Entity Governance Domains (Mandatory)

All **11** domains must pass.

---

## 1. üï∞ Temporal Abstraction & Coarse-Graining

Ensures:

- Sensitive events use **coarse time units** (e.g., decade/era/century)  
- No day/month/precise-year exposure for protected items  
- No derived precision beyond allowed granularity  

**Fail ‚Üí BLOCK**

---

## 2. üìè OWL-Time Masking Correctness

Ensures:

- `time:Instant` / `time:Interval` used safely  
- `time:hasBeginning` / `time:hasEnd` abstracted appropriately  
- No contradictory or overly precise interval semantics  

---

## 3. ü™∂ Ceremonial Cycle Protection (CARE-S)

Ensures:

- Sacred cycles (seasonal/ritual calendars) are **never** published in detail  
- No narrative inference of ceremonial frequency/patterns  
- No derived ‚Äúcultural time series‚Äù constructed for protected groups  

**Any violation ‚Üí IMMEDIATE BLOCK**

---

## 4. üß¨ Cultural Temporal Safety (Tribal Chronology)

Ensures:

- Tribal/Indigenous historical timelines appear only at safely generalized levels  
- No invented, speculative, or re-ordered cultural chronology  
- No unapproved historical assertions  

---

## 5. üß† Embedding Temporal Leakage Prevention

Ensures embeddings cannot:

- Encode exact temporal patterns of protected entities  
- Support clustering that reconstructs hidden timelines  

---

## 6. üåÄ Cluster-Based Temporal Reconstruction Blocking

Ensures clusters cannot:

- Group events into protected cultural cycles  
- Reveal latent ‚Äútribal time bands‚Äù  

---

## 7. üö® Anomaly Temporal Leakage Prevention

Ensures:

- Anomaly detection never flags protected temporal periods as ‚Äúunusual‚Äù in ways that reveal their character  
- No time-based anomaly labeling of protected events  

---

## 8. üìö Narrative Temporal Masking (SNv3 & FMv3)

Ensures:

- Story Node v3 narratives do not expose precise times for protected events  
- Focus Mode v3 cannot extrapolate or interpolate forbidden timelines  
- All narrative timelines follow masking rules  

---

## 9. üåê STAC/DCAT Temporal Metadata Alignment

Ensures:

- Dataset `datetime`, `start_datetime`, `end_datetime` are abstracted for protected data  
- FAIR+CARE temporal fields respect sovereignty  
- No mismatch between dataset metadata and narrative/graph masking  

---

## 10. üßæ PROV-O Temporal Provenance Safety

Ensures:

- PROV-O and OpenLineage timestamps for protected entities are generalized  
- No provenance chain stores precise ceremonial times  

---

## 11. üö¶ Promotion Gate v11 ‚Äî Temporal Sovereignty Criteria

Promotion requires:

- Complete temporal abstraction for protected entities  
- All OWL-Time structures sovereignty-safe  
- No drift-induced precision reintroduction  
- CARE-S validation & governance sign-off  

**Any violation ‚Üí Promotion BLOCKED**
